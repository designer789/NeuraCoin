How does NeuraCoin ensure data privacy and security?

+

NeuraCoin implements multiple layers of privacy protection. First, our federated learning approach allows models to be trained across distributed data sources without centralizing sensitive information. Second, we employ differential privacy techniques to prevent individual data identification. Third, we utilize zero-knowledge proofs and secure multi-party computation for applications requiring computation across private datasets. Finally, our governance includes clear data usage policies and consent mechanisms that give users control over how their data is utilized.
